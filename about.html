About Singh FC

Singh FC is a premier football team with a rich heritage, founded in 1995. Over the years, we have achieved significant milestones, including winning the national championship multiple times. Our commitment to excellence, both on and off the pitch, has garnered us a reputation as one of the most successful football clubs in the region.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized football team known for our passion, excellence, and commitment to community development.

Our Mission

To inspire and unite our community through the beautiful game of football, while nurturing the next generation of football stars.
